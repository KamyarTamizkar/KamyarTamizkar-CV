🔍 About Me

Research-driven geophysicist with experience in experimental fault mechanics, seismic interpretation, and subsurface geomechanics. Skilled in high-velocity laboratory simulations using SHIVA and analytical techniques like SEM and micro-Raman spectroscopy. My research focuses on understanding fault behavior in carbonate rocks, particularly under conditions relevant to seismic slip and CO₂ storage safety.

📊 Research Interests

Fault dynamics, high-pressure rock deformation, seismic slip processes, waveform analysis, attenuation (Q), full waveform inversion, induced seismicity, and geomechanics. Additional interests include seismic hazard assessment, rupture modeling, and the role of CO₂ storage in climate mitigation.
